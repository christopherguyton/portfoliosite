Plastic Villains

Clockwise from Left: Jerry Michael Sypkens, Mike O' Donnell, Chris Guyton, Todd Andersen, Alex Lozano

Plastic Villains was a five-piece grundrock band based in San Francisco.
Founded by Todd Andersen and Mike O'Donnell in 2010, Chris Guyton (credited as Chris Locs) joined the band in 2011 and played with them until the group split in 2013.
