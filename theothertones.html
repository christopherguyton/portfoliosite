THE OTHER TONES

Left To Right: Jaemi Fortier, Alex Lozano, Chris Guyton

The Other Tones was a three piece rock band formed in San Francisco and active between 2014-2018.
The group was founded by Chris Guyton (credited as Chris Locs), and Jaemi Fortier. Drummer Alex Lozano is featured on the bands debut album, "First Will and Testament" and was replaced later by Brian Davy until the band split completely in 2018
